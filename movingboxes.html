Free Moving Boxes

A simple local noticeboard to help people find and offer free moving boxes.

Looking for boxes?

  • Local pickup only.
  • Please be polite and timely.
  • Offers change quickly — check back often.

Want to offer boxes? Use the button on the right — it only asks for first name, street name, box count and size.

Have boxes to give away?

Click the button to submit an offer.

Note: Emails are sent via Netlify Forms Notifications (set to adralba@icloud.com in your Netlify site settings).
