Moving house? Renovating? Downsizing? This page helps people locally share moving boxes instead of throwing them out.

Have boxes? Let others know. Need boxes? Ask here and someone nearby might help.

I have boxes

If you have spare boxes after a move, renovation, or delivery, please let the community know.

Use the contact form and include:

  • Your suburb / area
  • How many boxes
  • Approximate size (small / medium / large)
  • When they are available

I need boxes

Looking for boxes nearby? Post what you need and where you are.

Use the contact form and include:

  • Your suburb / area
  • How many boxes you need
  • When you need them by

Why this exists

Thousands of good moving boxes go into recycling or landfill every week. This simple page exists to help people connect locally, save money, reduce waste, and help each other out.

Share this page in your local Facebook groups, community pages, and neighbourhood chats. The more people who know about it, the better it works.
